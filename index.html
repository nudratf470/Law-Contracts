Our Story

Two Decades of Commercial Legal Excellence

Founded in 2005, Law Contracts Ltd was established with a clear mission: to provide businesses with the kind of sharp, commercially-minded legal advice that was previously only available to the largest corporations.

We believe that great legal advice goes beyond the letter of the law. It must be practical, timely, and aligned with your commercial objectives. That philosophy is at the heart of everything we do.

Commercial Contracts

Watertight contracts are the foundation of every successful business relationship. We ensure yours protect you at every turn.

Overview

Contracts That Work as Hard as You Do

Every business relationship involves risk. Whether you are entering into a new supplier agreement, a distribution deal, or a complex joint venture, having a properly drafted and reviewed contract is essential to protecting your position and managing that risk.

At Law Contracts Ltd, our commercial contracts team has extensive experience across a wide range of industries and contract types. We do not produce generic, off-the-shelf documents — we take the time to understand your business, your objectives, and the specific deal at hand before drafting or advising.

Our approach is practical and commercial. We identify the issues that matter most to your business and focus our advice accordingly, giving you clear, actionable guidance rather than lengthy legal caveats.

What We Cover

Supply and procurement agreements
Distribution and agency contracts
Service level agreements (SLAs)
Confidentiality and NDA agreements
Joint venture and partnership agreements
IT and technology contracts
Franchise agreements
Terms & Conditions (B2B and B2C)
Get a Quote

Our Approach

How We Protect Your Interests

Drafting & Preparation

We draft contracts from scratch tailored precisely to your transaction, incorporating the protections your business needs and avoiding clauses that would disadvantage you.

Review & Due Diligence

Received a contract from a counterparty? We review it thoroughly, highlight risks and unfavourable terms, and advise you on how to respond or push back.

Negotiation Support

We act on your behalf in contract negotiations, or coach you through them, ensuring the final document reflects a fair and balanced commercial position.

Template Libraries

We develop bespoke contract templates for clients who regularly enter into similar agreements, saving you time and ensuring consistency across your business.

Our Process

From Instruction to Completion

1

Initial Consultation

We discuss your requirements, the nature of the transaction, and your key commercial objectives. This is free and without obligation.

2

Scoping & Quotation

We provide a clear, fixed-fee quotation so you know exactly what the work will cost before we begin.

3

Drafting or Review

We draft your contract or review the counterparty's document, identifying all key issues and producing a clean, annotated version.

4

Negotiation & Finalisation

We handle or support the negotiation process and ensure the final signed contract is in your best interests.

Corporate Law

From incorporation to exit, we provide the corporate legal expertise your business needs at every stage of its journey.

Overview

Supporting Your Business at Every Stage

Whether you are setting up a new company, restructuring an existing business, bringing in investors, or planning a sale or acquisition, the corporate decisions you make today will shape your business for years to come. Getting the right legal advice at the right time is critical.

Our corporate law team has advised businesses through every kind of corporate transaction and challenge, from straightforward company formations to complex multi-party mergers and acquisitions with cross-border elements. We bring the kind of practical, deal-focused expertise you would expect from a much larger firm, but with the personal service and competitive pricing of a specialist boutique.

We take the time to understand your business structure, your shareholder relationships, and your long-term ambitions, so that every piece of advice we give is truly tailored to where you are and where you want to go.

Commercial Property

Whether you are buying, selling, leasing, or developing commercial property, our specialist team handles the complexity so you can focus on your business.

Overview

Protecting Your Property Interests

Commercial property transactions are among the most significant decisions a business will ever make. Whether you are acquiring new premises, negotiating a lease for your office or retail space, or undertaking a development project, the legal complexities involved require specialist knowledge and careful attention to detail.

Our commercial property team advises landlords and tenants, buyers and sellers, investors and developers. We have extensive experience across office, retail, industrial, and mixed-use properties throughout England and Wales.

We work closely with you to understand your business objectives and timeline, ensuring that transactions are completed efficiently and that your interests are fully protected at every stage — from the initial heads of terms through to completion and beyond.

Commercial property purchases and sales
Commercial leases (landlord and tenant)
Lease renewals and surrenders
Property due diligence
Development agreements
Property finance and secured lending
Licences for alterations

Our Approach

Expert Guidance at Every Step

Acquisitions & Disposals

We manage the full purchase or sale process — from initial due diligence through to exchange and completion — ensuring a smooth and secure transaction.

Lease Negotiation

We draft, review, and negotiate commercial leases on behalf of both landlords and tenants, ensuring the terms are fair, balanced, and properly protect your position.

Development Projects

Property Investment
